Points & Badges System

Earn rewards and recognition for your contributions

Earn Rewards & Recognition

Understand our gamification system and learn how to earn points, unlock badges, and climb the leaderboards.

How to Earn Points

  • Creating snippets: +10 points per snippet
  • Receiving likes: +2 points per like
  • Getting shares: +5 points per share
  • Daily login: +5 points per day
  • Quality contributions: +20 points for high-quality content
  • Helping others: +15 points for answering questions

Available Badges

  • Newcomer: Create your first snippet
  • Code Creator: Create 10 snippets
  • Popular Contributor: Receive 100 total likes
  • Expert Developer: Create 50 snippets
  • Community Helper: Help 25 users
  • Quality Champion: Maintain 4.5+ average rating
  • Speed Runner: Create 5 snippets in one day
  • Knowledge Seeker: View 100 snippets

Leaderboards

  • Weekly Leaders: Top contributors this week
  • Monthly Champions: Best performers this month
  • All-Time Legends: Highest scoring users overall
  • Category Masters: Top contributors by language/field

Benefits of Points & Badges

  • Unlock premium features and themes
  • Get priority support and assistance
  • Access to exclusive beta features
  • Special recognition in the community
  • Opportunities to become a community mentor
